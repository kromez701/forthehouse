What can I clean with the Skirting Board Cleaner?

It’s ideal for skirting boards, door frames, stair rails, fireplace mantels, and even crown moulding. Basically, any low or high surface that’s tricky to reach.

Can I use it dry, or do I need to wet it?

You can use it either way. Use it dry for everyday dusting, or dampen the pad for a proper clean when there's more grime to clean.

How do I clean the microfiber pad?

Just rinse it under water or pop it in the wash. It’s reusable, so no need for disposable wipes.

Will it scratch painted or delicate surfaces?

Not at all. The microfiber pad is soft enough for painted and polished surfaces.
